Nathaniel Thompson, who is also known as giggs, is an influentual singer and songwriter from Peckham, London, Giggs was born on the 11th May 1981. He has been around the music scene since 2008 releasing an album at the time but had been active even further back starting his music in 2005. Giggs also goes by another stage name as Hollowman. The first album previously mentioned was 'Walk in da Park' and had a follow up album of 'Let Em Ave It'. The next album wasn't quite recognised as it was quite aggressive and a bit hard to swallow when listening too, 'When Will It Stop'. The fourth album reached the UK Albums Chart peaking at number 2, this became the highest charting album of his work to date.

Harrison Armstrong who is also known as Aitch was born in December 9th 1999 who is a British rapper and songwriter from Manchester. Aitch first came about during 2016 releasing videos on social media to gain attention. His solo breakthrough 'Straight Rhymez' gethered over 20 million views and gained him the attention of the rapper Stormzy'

Jermaine Sinclaire Scott is born 9th March 1985, he is also known as Wretch 32. Wretch is a songwriter and singer from Tottenham, North London. He was a memeber of a grime collective 'Combination Chain Gang' he then moved on to create 'The movement with another artist 'Scorcher, Ghetts and Mercston'. Wretch has been around since 2006 and is on par with reputation such as 'Giggs'. In 2011 three of the top five charting songs were held by Wretch which were from his debut album 'Black and White'
